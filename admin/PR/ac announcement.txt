Subject: VOTE by 2020-10-30: Publication Manifest and Audiobooks are W3C Proposed Recommendations
---
Dear Advisory Committee Representative,
Chairs,

W3C is pleased to announce the advancement of Publication Manifest and Audiobooks specifications to Proposed Recommendations:
  https://www.w3.org/TR/2020/PR-pub-manifest-20201001/
  https://www.w3.org/TR/2020/PR-audiobooks-20201001/

Please review the specifications and indicate whether you endorse them as W3C Recommendations or object to the advancement by completing the following questionnaire:
  https://www.w3.org/2002/09/wbs/33280/audiobooks-pr/

The deadline for responses is 03:59 UTC/GMT on 2020-10-31 (23:59 Boston time on 2020-10-30).

The approval and publication are in response to this transition request:
  https://github.com/w3c/transitions/issues/266

Future updates to these specifications may incorporate new features, following
  https://www.w3.org/2020/Process-20200915/#allow-new-features

Additional details about the review are available in the questionnaire. 

More information about the Publishing Working Group is available on the group home page:
  https://www.w3.org/publishing/groups/publ-wg/

If you should have any questions or need further information, please contact Ivan Herman <ivan@w3.org>, Publishing Working Group Team Contact.

This Call for Review follows section 6.2.9 of the W3C Process Document:
  https://www.w3.org/2020/Process-20200915/#transition-pr
